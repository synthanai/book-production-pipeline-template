## üìä Audiobook Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Format:** M4B (audiobook) or MP3 chapters
- **Bit Rate:** 128-192 kbps
- **Sample Rate:** 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Mono or Stereo:** Typically mono for spoken word
- **Peak Levels:** -3dB to -6dB (no clipping)

### Metadata
- Book title
- Author name
- Narrator name(s)
- Chapter titles
- Total runtime
- Cover art (square, 2400x2400 recommended)

## üí° Tips

### DIY Recording
- Invest in decent microphone (USB condenser mic)
- Record in quiet, acoustically treated space
- Use pop filter to reduce plosives
- Record in shorter sessions to maintain energy
- Take breaks to avoid vocal fatigue

### Professional Narration
- Hire experienced audiobook narrators
- Provide comprehensive narration guide
- Review sample recordings before full production
- Budget for post-production editing

### Distribution Platforms
- ACX (Audible/Amazon/iTunes)
- Findaway Voices
- Authors Republic
- Direct distribution
