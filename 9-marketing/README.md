## ğŸ¯ Marketing Best Practices

### Know Your Audience
- Define target reader precisely
- Understand their pain points
- Speak their language
- Meet them where they are

### Provide Value First
- Educational content before promotional
- Build trust through expertise
- Help first, sell second
- Create genuine connections

### Be Consistent
- Regular publishing schedule
- Consistent brand voice
- Persistent but not pushy
- Long-term thinking

### Leverage Multiple Channels
- Don't rely on single platform
- Cross-promote across channels
- Repurpose content efficiently
- Build owned audience (email list)
