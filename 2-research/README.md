## ğŸ“š Research Best Practices

### Citation and Sources
- Document all sources with full citations
- Keep research notes separate from original sources
- Use consistent citation format
- Track where ideas came from

### Organization
- Use clear folder structure
- Name files descriptively
- Create index or master research doc
- Cross-reference related research

### Synthesis
- Connect research to your concept
- Identify patterns and themes
- Note contradictions or debates
- Extract actionable insights

## ğŸ’¡ Tips

1. **Go deep** - Surface research shows in shallow writing
2. **Track sources** - You'll need them for citations and verification
3. **Look for gaps** - Gaps are opportunities for differentiation
4. **Stay organized** - Research can quickly become overwhelming
5. **Synthesize early** - Don't just collect, analyze and connect
6. **Know when to stop** - Research can be procrastination in disguise
