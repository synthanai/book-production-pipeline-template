## üìà Quality Metrics

**Marketing Effectiveness Indicators:**
- **Email list growth:** 50-100+ subscribers/month (goal-dependent)
- **Social media engagement:** Likes, comments, shares increasing
- **Website traffic:** 500-1,000+ visitors/month (goal-dependent)
- **Book sales:** Rank improvements on Amazon (goal: <100,000 rank)
- **Reviews:** 10-20+ reviews in first month, 50-100+ by 6 months
- **Media appearances:** 1-2 podcast interviews or guest posts/month
- **ROI:** If doing paid ads, track cost per sale (<$5-$10 ideal)

**Marketing Metrics to Track:**
- **Sales:** Daily/weekly/monthly book sales by format
- **Reviews:** Count and average rating on Amazon, Goodreads
- **Email list:** Subscriber count, open rate (15-25%), click rate (2-5%)
- **Social media:** Followers, engagement rate, reach
- **Website:** Traffic, bounce rate, time on site
- **Advertising:** Impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per acquisition

**Tools:**
- Amazon Author Central (sales dashboard)
- Google Analytics (website traffic)
- Email platform analytics (Mailchimp, ConvertKit)
- Social media insights (built-in analytics)
- Advertising dashboards (Amazon Ads, Facebook Ads Manager)

## üí° Tips for This Phase

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Start building email list EARLY (3-6 months before launch)
- ‚úÖ Focus on 2-3 marketing channels (don't spread too thin)
- ‚úÖ Create content consistently (weekly blog or email newsletter)
- ‚úÖ Engage with readers authentically (build relationships, not just sell)
- ‚úÖ Track metrics (know what's working, double down on winners)

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Wait until publication to start marketing (too late)
- ‚ùå Try every marketing channel at once (burnout + mediocrity)
- ‚ùå Only promote when you have something to sell (provide value regularly)
- ‚ùå Ignore metrics (marketing without measurement is guessing)
- ‚ùå Give up after launch week (marathon, not sprint)

**Common Pitfalls:**
- Waiting until launch to start marketing (missed opportunity)
- No email list (leaving money on the table)
- Inconsistent content (sporadic posting = lost momentum)
- Not tracking metrics (can't optimize what you don't measure)
- Quitting too early (marketing takes 6-12 months to compound)

**Minimum Viable Marketing:**
- Email list (start building 3-6 months pre-launch)
- Author website (simple, professional)
- One social platform (where your audience is)
- Regular content (blog or newsletter, weekly/bi-weekly)
- ARC program (20-50 beta readers for launch reviews)
- **Total time: 10-15 hours/month**
