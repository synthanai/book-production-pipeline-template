## üìà Quality Metrics

**Video Quality Indicators:**
- [ ] Professional video quality (HD 1080p minimum)
- [ ] Clear audio (no background noise)
- [ ] Good lighting (well-lit, not overexposed or too dark)
- [ ] Engaging visuals (not just talking head)
- [ ] Appropriate length (not too long, holds attention)
- [ ] Branded (consistent look/feel across videos)

**Platform Requirements:**
- **YouTube:** 1080p, 16:9 aspect ratio, thumbnail 1280x720px
- **Instagram Feed:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Instagram Stories/Reels:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **TikTok:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **LinkedIn:** 1080p, 16:9 aspect ratio

## üí° Tips for This Phase

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Start with simple videos (author intro, book pitch) before complex productions
- ‚úÖ Invest in decent microphone (audio quality matters more than video quality)
- ‚úÖ Use good lighting (natural light or ring light)
- ‚úÖ Keep videos concise (attention spans are short)
- ‚úÖ Batch filming (film multiple videos in one session)

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Obsess over perfection (done is better than perfect)
- ‚ùå Create videos you'll never publish (finish and ship)
- ‚ùå Ignore platform-specific formats (vertical for Instagram/TikTok)
- ‚ùå Forget SEO (titles, descriptions, keywords matter)
- ‚ùå Skip thumbnails (they drive clicks)

**Common Pitfalls:**
- Over-producing (spending weeks on a single video)
- Poor audio quality (audience will leave immediately)
- Videos too long (trim ruthlessly)
- Not optimizing for each platform
- Creating videos without distribution plan

**Minimum Viable Video Strategy:**
- 1 book trailer (1-2 minutes)
- 1 author introduction (2-3 minutes)
- 3-5 chapter preview clips (30-60 seconds each)
- **Total time: ~20-30 hours**
