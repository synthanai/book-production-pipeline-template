**Decision Needed: Publication Strategy**

### Traditional Publishing
**Process:** Query agents ‚Üí Agent submits to publishers ‚Üí Publisher acquires book ‚Üí Publisher handles production/distribution
**Timeline:** 1-3+ years from query to publication
**Pros:** Publisher handles everything, wider distribution, credibility
**Cons:** Very competitive, loss of control, lower royalties (10-15%)
**Best for:** Authors seeking validation, wide bookstore distribution, traditional path

### Self-Publishing
**Process:** Author handles everything ‚Üí Direct to platforms (Amazon KDP, etc.)
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from manuscript to live
**Pros:** Full control, higher royalties (35-70%), fast timeline
**Cons:** Author handles everything, harder to get bookstore distribution
**Best for:** Authors wanting control, speed, higher per-sale revenue

### Hybrid Publishing
**Process:** Mix of traditional and self-publishing ‚Üí Some services outsourced, some DIY
**Timeline:** 1-6 months depending on services
**Pros:** Professional help with key areas, still retain control
**Cons:** Upfront costs ($2K-$10K+), need to vet publishers carefully
**Best for:** Authors wanting professional polish with retained control

## üí° Tips for This Phase

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Invest in professional cover design (most important marketing asset)
- ‚úÖ Order print proof and review carefully before approval
- ‚úÖ Preview ebook on multiple devices
- ‚úÖ Research keywords and categories thoroughly (discoverability)

**Common Pitfalls:**
- Amateur-looking cover (instant turn-off for readers)

- No launch plan (book goes live to silence)
