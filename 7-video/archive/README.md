# archive/

**Purpose:** Archive for old video versions and unused footage

---

## What Goes Here

- Previous video edits
- Unused B-roll footage
- Old video scripts
- Previous trailer versions
- Deprecated promotional videos

## When to Archive

- Before major video re-edits
- When video scripts are revised
- When creating new versions
- When videos are updated for platforms
